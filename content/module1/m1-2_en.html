Colours and Icons

Overview

There are many factors to consider when developing a template. Presently accessibility is a driving factor behind many of the decisions being made. Colours need to have a high enough contrast to be visible to learners with low vision or colour blindness for example. Icons must be accompanied by descriptive text and have a simple design that is easily discerned.

The colours below are under construction

Colour palette key points:

  • Less colour is more
  • Colour can group similar content or indicate differences
  • Colour cannot be the only element used to convey information
  • Text and background need to have high contrast

For more information: Colour Guide
